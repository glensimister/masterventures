What We Do

As early investors of Bitcoin in 2012, the team behind Master Ventures has extensive experience across a range of sectors including venture capital, business development, software engineering, marketing and token economics. With a combined 20+ years of working knowledge within the blockchain space, the Master Ventures team are at the forefront of emerging technology and look to become a driving force behind the decentralization of existing models.

We offer high quality end-to-end advisory and incubation solutions to blockchain and early-stage technology projects on site or remotely.

Master Ventures provides financial and economic expertise to quality startups seeking CFO services by delivering strategic long term advisory for token based and hybrid (token and equity) Cryptocurrency startups.

End to End Marketing Advisory for 1E0 Campaign Launch

Business Model and Tokenomics

Expertise and consultation over token based or hybrid (token and equity) business model and ecosystem growth.

  • Market domination and user growth strategy
  • Development of token use cases within the platform
  • Token price growth and project monetisation strategy
  • User conversion and incentivization framework design
  • Store of value features and token liquidity management

Market benchmarked based token metrics and valuation model

  • Percentage structure of the tokens dedicated to each entity within the ecosystem
  • Token price behaviour simulation in regards to nodes, users, developers, the company, investors, miners, and others
  • Financial predictions and valuation (Milton Friedman Equation of Exchange - MV=PQ, Discounted Cash Flow, Market Comparisons, User Base Growth Simulation and more)
  • Sensitivity simulation
  • Visualization and summary

Fully aligned cryptocurrency market CFO services

  • Financial forecasting, budgeting, and reporting to the CEO and Board
  • Value and compensation distribution plan between equity and token holders
  • Financial performance measures and monitoring systems in regards to traditional and crypto finance strategy
  • Treasury management procedures for both, crypto and traditional finance assets

Dedicated spending and taxing strategy for impact on the ecosystem

  • Fee structure and ongoing updates based on the company’s revenue and token performance
  • Inflation rate structure and ongoing support
  • Projections and strategies to cryptocurrency market events and internal company situation
  • Interest rate structure based on the expected ROI of staking participants
